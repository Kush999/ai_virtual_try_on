Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TryOnAI

How does TryOnAI work?

TryOnAI uses GPT for intelligent prompt generation and NanoBanana for advanced image editing to analyze your uploaded photo and clothing items, then seamlessly blends them together to create realistic virtual try-on images. The process takes just a few seconds and produces high-quality results.

What types of clothing can I try on?

You can try on virtually any type of clothing including shirts, dresses, jackets, pants, and accessories. Our AI works best with clear, well-lit photos of clothing items.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes, absolutely. We take privacy seriously. Your photos are processed securely and are not stored permanently. We use industry-standard encryption and follow strict data protection protocols.

How long does it take to generate results?

Most virtual try-on images are generated within 10-30 seconds, depending on the complexity and number of images requested. Our AI is optimized for speed while maintaining high quality.

Can I download the generated images?

Yes! You can download individual images or all generated images at once. The images are provided in high resolution and are yours to keep.

What photo requirements do you have?

For best results, use clear, well-lit photos where you're facing the camera. Avoid photos with multiple people, heavy shadows, or blurry images. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, and WEBP up to 20MB.
